Teaching Statement

I focus on active, concept-driven teaching that supports students in understanding mathematical fundamentals and building confidence in problem solving. My teaching experience includes courses such as Calculus, Differential Equations, Linear Algebra, and Introductory Analysis.

My teaching approach includes:
- Designing examples that connect mathematics to data science and engineering applications.
- Encouraging collaborative problem solving and critical thinking.
- Providing mentorship for undergraduate research and project-based learning.

I aim to create a welcoming classroom environment where students from diverse backgrounds can succeed in mathematics and data science.
