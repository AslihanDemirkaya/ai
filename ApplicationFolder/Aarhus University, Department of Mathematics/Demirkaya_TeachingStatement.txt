Teaching Statement

I teach with a student-centered approach that emphasizes conceptual understanding, problem solving, and real-world applications. My experience includes teaching calculus, ordinary differential equations, linear algebra, introductory analysis, and advanced engineering mathematics at both undergraduate and graduate levels.

I support students by:
- Creating clear lecture notes and examples that connect theory to applications.
- Using active learning strategies in class and structured problem-solving sessions.
- Mentoring undergraduate research projects in nonlinear dynamics, numerical simulation, and mathematical modeling.

I am committed to inclusive teaching and enjoy supervising diverse students in mathematics and data science-related coursework.
