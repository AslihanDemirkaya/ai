Teaching Statement

I teach with clarity, curiosity, and a strong emphasis on connecting mathematical concepts to real applications. My instructional history includes courses such as Calculus, Differential Equations, Linear Algebra, and Advanced Engineering Mathematics.

My teaching principles include:
- Using real-world examples and computational exercises to make material relevant.
- Encouraging student participation through group work and guided problem solving.
- Mentoring students in research projects and helping them develop critical thinking skills.

I strive to create an inclusive learning environment where students build confidence in mathematics and apply quantitative reasoning to interdisciplinary challenges.
