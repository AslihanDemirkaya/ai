Research Statement

My research integrates applied mathematics, nonlinear PDEs, and data science. I have developed methods for analyzing complex systems, including stability of nonlinear waves and machine learning applications in classification and prediction.

Key areas:
- Nonlinear PDE stability and long-time behavior.
- Data science projects: fraud detection, image classification, regression modeling.
- Statistical modeling for scientific data.

At Academia Sinica, I would pursue research in applied statistics, collaborating on interdisciplinary projects and mentoring students in quantitative methods.
